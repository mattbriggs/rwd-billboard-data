# Context

The research goal is to study whether top-charting songs became less self-contained over time, and to do so without collapsing formal analysis into a vague judgment of "good" versus "bad" songwriting.

The software exists to support that workflow by:

- constructing a reproducible corpus
- separating chart context from lyric form
- extracting mechanical lyric features without default lyric redistribution
- supporting blind scoring against the Self-Containment Index rubric
- preserving provenance for chart, metadata, and lyric sources
